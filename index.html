About Me

Hello, I’m Philip, and I have rotated around the sun 19 times. I love making, exploring, and shaping the world into my ideal future for the benefit of humanity.



In my 6.1537*10^8 seconds of live, I self admittingly have done some kinda cool things. I have published 3 video games to the Apple App Store, Google Play Store, and Itch.io and created countless more imaginary digital worlds! I submitted to the top of Mount Kilimanjaro! I am mechanical engineer by trade, self-taught programmer, but hacker by heart. I am proud student Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology one of the finest undergraduate engineering schools in the world and a proud alumni Hack Club! I am pursuing a career where I will have substantial impact in perfecting our world for all people. I am extremely driven and hard worker with experience working in STEM education, venture capital, and academic research. I am extremely passionate about fighting climate change through innovative clean energy and transport solutions, equalizing STEM education for all secondary school students, and advancing humanity to the stars.
